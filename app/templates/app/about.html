Ideally: Organize and Grow Your Ideas

Are you creative and full of great ideas? Then Ideally is for you!

  • Ideally helps you keep track of your ideas
  • Ideally lets you organize and filter ideas by using tags
  • With Ideally you can grow your idea by adding milestones, photos, or notes
  • Ideally is simple and responsive and works on any device, wherever you are
  • Ideally is completely free and open source!
